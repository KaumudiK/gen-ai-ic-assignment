Acme Tech — Leave Policy

Annual Leave
Every full-time employee is entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per year.
Leaves are credited at the rate of 2 days per month. Unused leaves up to 12 days
can be carried forward to the next year.

Sick Leave
Employees get 12 days of paid sick leave annually. Sick leaves more than 2
consecutive days require a medical certificate. Sick leaves do not carry forward
to the next year.

Casual Leave
6 days of casual leave are provided per year for personal errands or short
breaks. Casual leaves cannot be combined with annual leave and cannot be carried
forward.

Maternity and Paternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave as per
government regulations. Male employees get 15 days of paid paternity leave that
can be taken within 6 months of the child's birth.

Bereavement Leave
In case of the loss of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child,
sibling), employees are entitled to 5 days of paid bereavement leave.

How to Apply
All leaves must be applied through the HR portal at least 3 working days in
advance, except for sick leave which can be applied retroactively. Manager
approval is mandatory for all leaves longer than 2 days.
