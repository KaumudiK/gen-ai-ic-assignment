Acme Tech — Work From Home Policy

Hybrid Model
Acme Tech follows a hybrid work model. Employees are required to come to the
office a minimum of 3 days per week. The remaining 2 days can be worked from
home. Tuesday and Thursday are mandatory office days for all teams.

Eligibility
All confirmed employees who have completed their 6-month probation period are
eligible for WFH. New joiners are expected to work from the office full-time
during their probation to facilitate learning and team integration.

Equipment
The company provides a laptop and a one-time setup allowance of Rs. 15,000 for
WFH equipment, which can be used for a monitor, ergonomic chair, or desk.
Receipts must be submitted within 60 days of joining.

Internet Allowance
Employees who work from home are eligible for an internet reimbursement of
Rs. 1,500 per month. This is automatically credited with the monthly salary upon
submission of the broadband bill.

Communication Expectations
WFH employees must be available on Slack and email during work hours. Cameras
should be turned on for all team meetings unless there's a specific reason not
to. Response time during work hours should not exceed 2 hours.

Full Remote Roles
Some roles are designated as fully remote. These employees are not required to
come to the office but must attend quarterly in-person team offsites at the
company's expense.
