Acme Tech — Employee Benefits Guide

Health Insurance
All employees and their immediate family (spouse, children, and parents) are
covered under our group health insurance policy. The sum insured is Rs. 5 lakhs
per family, with cashless treatment at over 5,000 hospitals across India.

Life Insurance
Every employee is covered under a term life insurance policy worth 3 times their
annual CTC. This is fully paid for by the company.

Provident Fund
12% of the basic salary is contributed to the Employee Provident Fund (EPF) each
month, with a matching contribution from the company. This amount can be
withdrawn after retirement or partially used for specific life events.

Wellness Programs
Acme Tech offers an annual wellness allowance of Rs. 25,000 per employee, which
can be used for gym memberships, yoga classes, mental health counseling, or
preventive health checkups.

Learning and Development
Employees get Rs. 30,000 per year for professional development. This can be
spent on online courses, books, conferences, or certifications. Approved courses
related to the employee's role are reimbursed at 100%.

Stock Options
Senior employees (Manager level and above) are eligible for stock options as
part of their compensation. Vesting happens over 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

Referral Bonus
Employees who successfully refer a new hire receive a bonus of Rs. 50,000,
payable after the new hire completes 6 months in the company.

Free Meals
The office cafeteria provides free breakfast, lunch, and snacks for all
employees on office days.
