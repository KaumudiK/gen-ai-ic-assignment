Acme Tech — General Company Policies

Office Hours
Our standard office hours are 9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday. We follow a
flexible timing policy where employees can start anywhere between 9:00 AM and
10:30 AM, as long as they complete 9 hours of work including a 1-hour lunch break.

Code of Conduct
All employees are expected to behave professionally and respectfully toward
colleagues, clients, and partners. Discrimination, harassment, or bullying of any
kind is strictly prohibited. Violations should be reported to HR immediately
through hr@acmetech.com or the anonymous reporting portal.

Dress Code
Acme Tech follows a smart-casual dress code. Formal attire is expected for client
meetings, board meetings, and external events. Friday is considered casual day.
T-shirts with offensive prints, shorts, and flip-flops are not allowed in the
office.

Performance Reviews
Employee performance is reviewed twice a year — once in June and once in
December. Reviews include self-assessment, peer feedback, and a manager
evaluation. Promotions and salary revisions happen during the December cycle.

Exit Policy
Employees wishing to resign must submit their resignation through the HR portal
and serve a 60-day notice period. During this time, employees are expected to
complete handovers and document their work. The final settlement is processed
within 45 days of the last working day.
